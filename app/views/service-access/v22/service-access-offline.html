What you can do instead

  1. Tell your GP surgery that you want to sign up for their online services. They'll ask you to fill in a form and prove who you are.
  2. Your GP surgery will give you registration details. You'll need:
    • Linkage Key (could be called a Passphrase)
    • O.D.S. Code (could be called an Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
    • Account I.D.
  3. When you have these 3 details, go back to {{ serviceName }} and use your NHS login.
