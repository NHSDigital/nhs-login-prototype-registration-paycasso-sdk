We need to connect your NHS login to your GP surgery

This is so that you can access your GP surgery's online services.

Online services let you order repeat prescriptions, and book appointments online.

If you've already asked to use your GP surgery's online services, they might have given you a letter with your registration details on it.

You'll need

  • Linkage Key (could be called a Passphrase)
  • O.D.S. Code (could be called an Organisation Code or Practice I.D.)
  • Account I.D.

You won't need

  • Username
  • Password
